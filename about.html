About Us

How Vivid Electrical Began

Lachlan Roberts began an apprenticeship at the age of 16, determined to create his own future and establish Vivid Electrical. By 2017, he earned the prestigious “Apprentice of the Year” title.

By the age of 20, Lachlan was a fully qualified solar electrician. With a dedication to mastering the trade with the entrepreneurial spirit that burned within him, he spent the next four years honing his skills under experienced professionals.

Gold Coast's Family-Owned Solar & Electrical Specialists

Lachlan's vision materialised into Vivid Electrical, now a thriving 100% Australian, family-owned, and operated business. Our team comprises accredited solar installers and skilled electricians dedicated to delivering tailored solar and electrical solutions to residential clients across the Gold Coast and surrounding areas.

In 2019, Lachlan's commitment to excellence in electrical craftsmanship was further underscored when he secured the bronze medal in the Electrical category at the Regional Competition for WorldSkills Australia. This achievement reflects our unwavering commitment to providing superior service while adhering to the highest safety standards.

Empowering Gold Coast Homes and Businesses with Clean Energy

We specialise in bespoke solar installations for residential and commercial properties, including advanced battery storage systems and solar repairs. We also offer additional solar services such as hot water timers and energy-efficient lighting to boost your energy savings. Our comprehensive site monitoring provides real-time insights into your energy usage and production, helping you make informed decisions.

Get Your Electrical Work Done Right

We offer a full range of electrical solutions for homes and businesses. Our electrical services include switchboard upgrades, smoke alarm installations, indoor and outdoor LED lighting, rewiring, electrical safety inspections, and electrical additions and maintenance. We also provide test and tagging to ensure your commercial equipment meets safety standards.
